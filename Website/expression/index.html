About Us

Dandenong Dolphins was established in 2010, a relatively new swim club located in the sporting province at JC Mills Reserve in Dandenong North. We wanted to create a club which brings people who love swimming together. At our club, we offer both recreational and competitve swimming classes, so there's something for everyone.
